Cancellation and Refund Policy

We strive to provide the best service to our users. However, if you wish to cancel a ticket or request a refund, please follow the steps below:

Cancellation

Tickets can be canceled within 24 hours of purchase for a full refund. Please contact us through the Contact Us page for assistance.

Refunds

If a refund is requested, we will process it within 7-10 business days. Refunds are subject to approval based on the circumstances of the request.
